Superellipse family — exponent sweep

n = 1 → 8
The Lame formula |x/a|^n + |y/b|^n = 1 produces a continuous family of shapes. n=1 is a diamond, n=2 a circle, n=4 the classic squircle, n→∞ a rectangle. Each shape here is exactly what you'd get by varying the exponent on a Raspberry Pi 5 case outline.
